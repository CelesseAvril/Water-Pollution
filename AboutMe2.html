About Us

We are a non-profit organization that aim to raise awarness about water pollution since water is a very important resources. In our country trash can be found in the water especially after it rains. We believe others should be aware of water pollution, how it affects the world, how the water is polluted,etc because if we continue this was we will not have any fresh or clean water left.
